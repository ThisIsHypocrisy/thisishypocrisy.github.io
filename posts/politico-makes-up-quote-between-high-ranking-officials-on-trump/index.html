Politico Makes Up Quote Between High Ranking Officials on Trump - NATO Spokesperson Demands Correction
Aug 26, 2018
  • Tweet
  • Share

Politico published an article in May 2017 claiming that the NATO Secretary General Jens Stoltenberg told a former senior NATO official that Trump has a “12-second attention span”. The author does not cite how she got this information.

Moments after the official Politico account tweeted the article, Oana Lungescu, an official NATO Spokesperson, responds to the tweet asking Politico to withdraw the claim given that Stoltenberg never made this statement.

Politico couldn’t defend the claim and was forced to make a correction within the article. The tweet quoting the claim was never removed.

It’s scary that the article author, who is supposed to be an unbiased and objective reporter, had decided to publish an unsubstantiated quote that has ramifications on the important relationship between the United States and NATO.
