The Japanese Government Disputes the Washington Post's Claim That Trump Brought Up Pearl Harbor in Trade Talks
Sep 1, 2018
  • Tweet
  • Share

On August 28th, The Washington Post published an article titled ” ‘I remember Pearl Harbor’: Inside Trump’s hot and cold relationship with Japan’s prime minister”. The article accused Trump of bringing up Pearl Harbor before launching into “a blistering critique of Japan’s economic policies”. No source was cited for the claim.

If the accusation was true, it’d make Trump appear to be a disrespectful and insensitive negotiator. Unfortunately for the Post, it’s not and the Japanese government even had to publicly dispute the claim.

It’s incredibly shoddy journalism for reporters to make unbacked claims that can harm important international relationships between two powerful countries. This is another example of reporters pushing a biased narrative at the expense of objective and fact-based journalism.
