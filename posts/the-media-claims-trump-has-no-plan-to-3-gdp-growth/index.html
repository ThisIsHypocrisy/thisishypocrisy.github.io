The Media Claims Trump Has No Plan To Reach 3% GDP Growth - and We Just Reached 4.1% GDP Growth

Aug 25, 2018

Donald Trump set a goal of 3% GDP growth. Since 2010, US’s GDP growth has been stagnating at around 2%. Soon after this goal was announced, the media quickly assumed a highly critical position. Here are some examples:

  • “If Trump thinks he can get more than 3% economic growth, he’s dreaming” - Michael Hiltzik, Los Angeles Times
  • “Can Trump Deliver 3% Growth? Stubborn Realities Stand in the Way” - Nick Timiraos and Andrew Tangel, The Wall Street Journal
  • “Sorry, Mr. Trump, but the only way to get to 3% growth is to hire more Mexicans” - Rex Nutting, MarketWatch
  • “Trump promises 4% growth. Economists say no way.” - Patrick Gillespie, CNN Money
  • “Why ‘MAGAnomics’ Isn’t Likely To Work” - Danielle Kurtzleben, NPR

Well, unfortunately for the media, the US has not only reached the 3% GDP growth goal, it surpassed it to reach a spectacular 4.1% GDP growth in Q2 of 2018.
