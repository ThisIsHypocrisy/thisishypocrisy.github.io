    NBC, Amazon, And Cartoon Network Writer Asks For White Extinction
    • Tweet
    • Share

    On June 25th, Taylor Cox, a writer for many children’s TV shows including Just Add Magic, Swamp Talk with Shrek & Donkey, OMG!, and Kung Fu Panda tweeted this statement: “the only anxiety i have about white Extinction is that it’s not happening fast enough”.

    Cox has since deleted the tweet and took her Twitter account private.

    For a progressive, it’s surprising that she has convinced herself that it’s okay to publicly ask for the extinction of an entire human race. I guess, when your need to virtue signal overcomes critical thinking, this is the result.
