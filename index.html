This Is Hypocrisy

  • Keeping Corporate Media Accountable

This Is Hypocrisy archives every instance of hypocrisy from the mainstream media. Many media companies stretch and bend reality to spin a story, and sometimes they get caught. To keep them accountable, we plan to archive every instance of hypocrisy to understand the extent of the deception.
